Video operations
aliasattributeRequired?valueDescription
NoneslotnoBuffer number (from 0)Specify the buffer number.
Default is 0.
Nonefornoloop or period or prepare or segLoopSpecifies the type of event to wait for.
Specifying loop will wait for the end of the loop.

If you specify segLoop, it will wait for the end of the segment loop.
